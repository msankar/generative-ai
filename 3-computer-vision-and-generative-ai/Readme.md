# Diffusion Models - Course 5 of Udacity ND608

## Course Overview
This course focuses on diffusion models, a class of generative models that have gained popularity for their ability to generate high-quality data. The course covers the theoretical foundations, practical implementations, and applications of diffusion models in various domains.

## Learning Objectives
By the end of this course, you will be able to:
- Understand the principles behind diffusion models and their significance in generative AI.
- Implement diffusion models using popular libraries such as PyTorch and TensorFlow.
- Experiment with different architectures and hyperparameters to optimize model performance.
- Apply diffusion models to real-world datasets and evaluate their effectiveness.

## Course Structure
The course is divided into several modules, including:
1. **Introduction to Diffusion Models**: Understanding the basics and historical context.
2. **Mathematics of Diffusion Processes**: Exploring the mathematical foundations.
3. **Implementing Diffusion Models**: Hands-on coding exercises using Jupyter Notebooks.
4. **Applications of Diffusion Models**: Case studies and practical applications in various fields.
5. **Advanced Topics**: Exploring cutting-edge research and developments in diffusion models.

## Prerequisites
To get the most out of this course, you should have:
- A basic understanding of machine learning concepts.
- Familiarity with Python programming.
- Experience with deep learning frameworks such as PyTorch or TensorFlow.
